**Objective:** Prove MalSat is the default livestock platform in Kyrgyzstan and validate B2B + SaaS as the scale engines.

**Annual KRs:**
- 50,000 registered farmers by end-FY26
- $300K MRR by end-FY26
- 30% of digitally-listed KG livestock sold through MalSat

- ≥3 B2B anchor contracts signed (supermarket / caterer / processor)

**Objective:** Become the livestock OS of 3 Central Asian countries and open the FinTech + Export revenue layers.

**Annual KRs:**

- 1st halal export deal closed (≥$2M GMV) via MalSat Global

- **Monday** — 30-min OKR check-in (status + blockers)
